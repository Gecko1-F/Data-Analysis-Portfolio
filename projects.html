01

Amazin Mart Sales Performance Dashboard

📊 Tableau 2026 3 days

🎯 Business Case

A retail company with regional offices had declining visibility into their sales performance. Leadership couldn't identify underperforming segments in real-time.

📊 Dataset

  • Source: github.com/pavanjuturu/Tableau/blob/master/AmazingMartEU2.xlsx
  • Size: 4,118 transactions
  • Period: 2011–2014
  • Fields: 10 columns including Order Date, Customer Name, City, Country, Region, Segment, Ship Date, Ship Mode

🔧 Approach & Process

  1. Data Cleaning: Removed 47 duplicate records and standardized date formats using SQL
  2. Data Modeling: Created calculated fields for profit margin & YoY growth
  3. Dashboard Design: Built interactive dashboard with filters for region, category, and time period
  4. Insights Generation: Identified key trends and formulated actionable recommendations

💡 Key Findings

  • The United Kingdom's corporate profit growth outperformed Germany's by 4.76%
  • Storage sub-category showed highest growth (70.12% on average YoY) with a margin of 8%
  • Consumer segment represented 52% of revenue with a 12% of profit margin
  • Overall, the Tables sub-category has been a disaster, decreasing by 111% year-over-year on average, with its worst drop occurring in 2012

🛠️ Tools Used

Tableau Desktop SQL Excel Data Cleaning

02

Obesity Levels Based on Physical Activity and Nutritional Habits

📈 Power BI March 2025 3 days

🎯 Business Case

An organization with 1,470 employees was experiencing unexplained attrition. HR needed a way to identify which departments and demographics had the highest turnover risk.

📊 Dataset

  • Source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/fatemehmehrparvar/obesity-levels
  • Size: 2,111 unique records
  • Fields: 17 attributes including gender, weight, age, high calorie intake, etc.

🔧 Approach & Process

  1. Cleaned and transformed data using Power Query
  2. Built DAX measures for Weight Status, BMI, Age Groups and other key metrics.
  3. Created a multi-page report with executive summary, LIFESTYLE IMPACT, and demographic analysis

💡 Key Findings

  • Remote workers had 34% higher retention rate
  • Employees with <2 years tenure had 3x attrition rate
  • Sales department had highest turnover at 21%

🛠️ Tools Used

Power BI DAX Power Query

03

Lamborghini Sales 2020 - 2025

📉 Looker Studio 2025

🎯 Business Case

Lamborghini's demand patterns (order volumes, wait times, pricing power) serve as a leading indicator of ultra-high-net-worth (UHNW) consumer confidence and capital deployment behavior. A recession typically manifests in UHNW spending hesitation 6–12 months before official macro indicators (yield curve inversion, unemployment data, GDP contraction) confirm economic slowdown. The organization needs to develop a predictive framework that uses Lamborghini demand elasticity data to identify recession signals early enough to execute defensive portfolio repositioning.
