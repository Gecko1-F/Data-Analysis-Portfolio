01

Amazin Mart Sales Performance Dashboard

📊 Tableau January 2025 2 weeks

🎯 Business Problem

A retail company with 4 regional offices had declining visibility into their sales performance. Monthly reports took 3 days to compile manually from Excel spreadsheets, and leadership couldn't identify underperforming segments in real-time.

📊 Dataset

  • Source: Kaggle Superstore Dataset (modified)
  • Size: 9,994 transactions
  • Period: 2020–2023
  • Fields: 21 columns including sales, profit, region, category, customer segment

🔧 Approach & Process

  1. Data Cleaning: Removed 47 duplicate records and standardized date formats using SQL
  2. Data Modeling: Created calculated fields for profit margin, YoY growth, and running totals
  3. Dashboard Design: Built interactive dashboard with filters for region, category, and time period
  4. Insights Generation: Identified key trends and formulated 3 actionable recommendations

💡 Key Findings

  • West region outperformed others by 23% in profit margin
  • Technology category showed highest growth (31% YoY) but lowest margin (12%)
  • Consumer segment represented 52% of revenue but only 41% of profit
  • Q4 consistently showed 2.3x average daily sales vs. Q1

🛠️ Tools Used

Tableau Desktop SQL Excel Data Cleaning

02

HR Analytics Dashboard

📈 Power BI February 2025 3 weeks

🎯 Business Problem

An organization with 1,470 employees was experiencing unexplained attrition. HR needed a way to identify which departments and demographics had the highest turnover risk.

📊 Dataset

  • Source: IBM HR Analytics Employee Attrition Dataset
  • Size: 1,470 employee records
  • Fields: 35 attributes including satisfaction scores, tenure, department, salary

🔧 Approach & Process

  1. Cleaned and transformed data using Power Query
  2. Built DAX measures for attrition rate, average tenure, and satisfaction indices
  3. Created a multi-page report with executive summary, departmental drill-down, and demographic analysis

💡 Key Findings

  • Remote workers had 34% higher retention rate
  • Employees with <2 years tenure had 3x attrition rate
  • Sales department had highest turnover at 21%

🛠️ Tools Used

Power BI DAX Power Query

03

Lamborghini Sales 2020 - 2025

📉 Looker Studio 2025

🎯 Business Case

Lamborghini's demand patterns (order volumes, wait times, pricing power) serve as a leading indicator of ultra-high-net-worth (UHNW) consumer confidence and capital deployment behavior. A recession typically manifests in UHNW spending hesitation 6–12 months before official macro indicators (yield curve inversion, unemployment data, GDP contraction) confirm economic slowdown. The organization needs to develop a predictive framework that uses Lamborghini demand elasticity data to identify recession signals early enough to execute defensive portfolio repositioning.
